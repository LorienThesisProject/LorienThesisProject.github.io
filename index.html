Refugee Voices of Colorado

Below, you will find the stories of individuals who have come to Colorado to seek refuge. Many of them are students at the Immigrant and Refugee Center of Northern Colorado, mixed with others who provide a perspective of those who have been in Colorado for much of their lives. These individuals volunteered to share their stories to quench the curiosity of community members, to dispell myths that may exist about refugees, and even to have someone to listen and recognize that what they have to offer is valid. Journey forth with an open mind, clicking each story to learn about the individuals and what they have to share.
