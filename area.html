In Greeley, many of the refugees are Somali and East African. Many more are also Burmese, from varying ethnic groups. Greeley is also poised uniquely, as 57% of people in the surrounding county are Trump voters - a demographic that is often at odds with the growing refugee population. Though the largest employer in the areais JBS Swift, offering job that allow refugees in the area, the workplace is not known historically of being supportive of its workers - over 100 of them were fired in 2008 over a disagreement on Ramadan prayer accommodations (Brasch). It is a community full of refugees, but not always supportive of refugees.
